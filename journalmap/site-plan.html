Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a platform for those who wnt to remember where theyve been. It will literally be a map of your life! It will allow users to note memories, add pictures and capture the moment right where it happened.

Audience

The platform is intended for any user who wants to keep their precious moments though it will probably be most used by age ranging from 8-60. From a group of young friends hanging out to parents who want to capture their babies first steps, even grandparents who want to remember where their keys are.

Content

Your Map

How to use: Welcome to the Map Journal, the place for all your personal moments, pictrues, and thoughts! Below is your personal Journal Map, a road to your life. The purpose of the journal map is to take all the the amazing technology we have at our fingeer tips and didgitize your own journal. This will make it easier and faster to capture the moemnts of your life you dont want to forget! On Your Map Journal add a thought, a full journal entry and pictures to go right when you are there or just mark the location for adding the entry later, its up to you! Main Content: The map used to mark/enter entries.

Images for the Home page

Your Journal

A journal is only as effective as it is kept, so this page is meant for your long form entries. A literal journal, you can add full storys and upload pictures that you took that are apart of the moment or just customize it to be how you want it!

Your Memories

With each entry, picture, and moment you add, the more you build a road map of your life. So why not see it that way? Follow your life in chronological order on a "road map" of your Life. Use your arrow keys to move forward and read your entries and remmeber each moment you captured.
